The Challenge

Atrial fibrillation is a silent epidemic with devastating consequences

59M+ People affected worldwide
5x Increased stroke risk
20% Of all strokes caused by AF
Often Undiagnosed until complications

Limitations in Existing Solutions

  • Manual ECG interpretation is time-intensive and subject to inter-reader variability
  • Short recording windows reduce detection of intermittent AF episodes
  • Limited access to specialist cardiology services in remote or underserved regions
  • Delayed detection reduces opportunities for preventive intervention

Our Solution

A three-stage AI-powered pipeline combining portable devices with explainable intelligence

1

ECG Acquisition

Portable ECG data collection using the Bittium Faros 360 device (FDA 510(k) and CE Class IIa approved) for continuous, medical-grade cardiac monitoring

2

Signal Processing

ECG waveforms converted into standardized 2D images through sophisticated preprocessing pipelines, creating consistent input for AI analysis

3

AI Classification

Fine-tuned LLaMA 3.2-11B vision-language model analyzes ECG images and generates both AF predictions and clinical explanations in natural language

Key Innovation

Unlike black-box AI systems, our model provides explainable reasoning that aligns with clinical ECG criteria, helping clinicians understand why AF was detected
